ABOUT TANOW ONLINE

Your trusted guide to global live television streaming

Our Mission

TaNow Online was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make global television accessible to everyone. In a world where streaming services are often fragmented and geo-restricted, we believe that publicly available television content should be easy to discover and access. Our platform serves as a comprehensive directory, organizing thousands of IPTV channels from around the world into a user-friendly, searchable database.

What We Do

We aggregate information about publicly available IPTV streams from across the globe. Our platform provides:

  • Comprehensive Coverage: Channels from over 200 countries, spanning news, sports, entertainment, music, and more
  • Easy Discovery: Browse by country, category, or use our powerful search feature
  • Built-In Player: Watch streams directly in your browser without additional software
  • Regular Updates: Our database is continuously updated to maintain accuracy

Our Data Source

TaNow Online is powered by the IPTV-ORG open-source project, one of the largest community-maintained collections of publicly available IPTV streams. This collaboration ensures that our directory remains comprehensive, accurate, and up-to-date.

Important Information

TaNow Online is a directory service only. We do not:

  • Host, upload, or distribute any video content
  • Own or operate any television channels
  • Store any streams on our servers
  • Modify or re-encode any video content

All streams listed on our platform are publicly available on the internet and are hosted by third parties. We simply organize and present links to this content in an accessible format.

Legal Streaming

We are committed to promoting legal streaming practices. Our directory focuses on:

  • Free-to-air television channels
  • Publicly broadcast content
  • Official network streams
  • Government and educational channels

We encourage all users to respect copyright laws and support content creators through official channels when possible.

For Content Owners

If you are a content owner or representative and have concerns about any listing on our platform, please contact us immediately. We take copyright concerns seriously and will promptly investigate and address any legitimate claims.
