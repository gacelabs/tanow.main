FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about TaNow Online

What is TaNow Online?
TaNow Online is a comprehensive IPTV channel directory that aggregates and organizes links to publicly available television streams from around the world. We provide an easy-to-use interface for discovering and watching live TV channels from various countries and categories.
Is TaNow Online free to use?
Yes, TaNow Online is completely free to use. We display advertisements to support the service, but you don't need to pay anything or create an account to browse and watch channels.
Do you host the video streams?
No, TaNow Online does not host any video content. We are a directory service that organizes and provides links to streams that are publicly available on the internet. All streams are hosted by third parties, typically the official broadcasters or their CDN providers.
Why isn't a channel working?
Streams can become unavailable for various reasons: the broadcaster may have changed their stream URL, there could be temporary technical issues, or the stream may be geo-restricted in your region. If a channel isn't working, try again later or explore similar channels from the same category.
How do I watch a channel?
Simply click on any channel card to open our built-in video player. The stream will load automatically in your browser. No additional software or plugins are required. Our player supports HLS streams, which work on most modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
Is it legal to watch these streams?
The streams listed on TaNow Online are from publicly available sources, including free-to-air channels, official broadcaster streams, and public access channels. However, the legality of accessing certain content may vary depending on your jurisdiction. We encourage users to respect copyright laws and support official content providers.
How can I report a copyright issue?
If you are a copyright owner and believe that content linked on our site infringes your rights, please contact us through our contact page. We take copyright concerns seriously and will promptly investigate and address any legitimate claims.
Where does the channel data come from?
Our channel database is powered by the IPTV-ORG open-source project, a community-maintained collection of publicly available IPTV streams. This collaboration ensures our directory remains comprehensive and regularly updated.
Can I use TaNow Online on mobile devices?
Yes! TaNow Online is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Our mobile-first design ensures a great experience on any device. The video player also works on mobile browsers that support HLS playback.
How often is the channel list updated?
Our channel data is fetched directly from the IPTV-ORG API, which is updated regularly by the community. This means you always have access to the latest channel information and stream URLs without needing to refresh anything manually.
